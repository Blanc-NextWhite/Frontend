TF2Stadium Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide a place for players of all skill levels to play competitive TF2 and to offer a welcoming place of learning for those new to the community.

This organisation aims to be as transparent and democratic as possible in its operations. Therefore, all code is open source and freely available to the public. The site itself will remain free of charge for all users.
