Punishment Guidelines and Repeat Offenders

As a user has repeat violations the severity of the punishment will increase. For example, a first time chat violation may lead to a 30 minute chat ban while a third may be a week ban. Rule violations after the third offense or situations that require extra attention are escalated to an Admin Committee that may determine to proceed as they feel necessary.

Staff Structure

We have 3 different levels of staff: Trial Moderator, Moderator, and Administrator. Each of these three have access to slightly different sets of tools. Trial Moderators can ban a user up to 1 week, Moderators can ban up to 1 month, and Administrators can ban up to 3 months. Any bans beyond that can only be given out by a committee of Administrators that meet regularly.

Appeal Process

Our staff are human and humans make mistakes sometimes. We realize this and take our appellate process just as seriously as our moderation. If you believe you were punished unjustly and wish to appeal, follow the stickied instructions in our Ban Appeals Forum.

If you believe a staff member is abusing their power, please message a Moderator or an Administrator you feel comfortable talking to. We treat cases of staff abuse with the utmost seriousness and will take all action necessary to stop such activities. However please realize that it is impossible for any staff to always take action in a way that all parties feel is fair or even is objectively fair.

Questions, Comments, and Feedback

If you have any questions, issues, or concerns with our rules or how we handle rule violations, please post in our Rules, Questions, and Feedback Forum or contact a staff member.
